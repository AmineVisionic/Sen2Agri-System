Sentinel-2 for Agriculture system is designed to automatically generate 
key products for agriculture monitoring, based on Sentinel-2 and 
Landsat-8 data (cloud-free surface reflectance composite, dynamic cropland 
mask, cultivated crop type map and vegetation indicators describing the 
vegetative development of crops).
